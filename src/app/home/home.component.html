Welcome to The Hive!

Keep your business alive with help from The Hive!

The Hive is an automotive fleet management software application. It provides real time vehicle intelligence and analytics for any business that operates a fleet of vehicles. The up-to-the-minute nature of the Hive is especially useful for fleet operators in industries such as:

  • Emergency services
  • Transportation
  • Shipping and logistics
  • Utility operators

In addition to real-time data, the Hive also provides dashboards to drive improvements in the operational efficiency of your fleet. These dashboards track metrics such as vehicle breakdowns, vehicle miles traveled, and other vehicle activities.
